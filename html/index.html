Maginet is a turn-based strategy game where two guilds of mages battle each other.

Mages can move to any of the adjacent tiles they are standing on.

After moving, they cast a spell with their individual pattern, damaging the enemies in its path.

Once low on health, mages charge up, allowing them to travel to the adjacent diagonals too!

Mages have limited mana, once drained, they will go to sleep. You cannot move over a sleeping mage.
